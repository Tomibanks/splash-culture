About Us

At Splash, we are dedicated to making a splash in the world of swimming! We offer a comprehensive range of services, including swim lessons, pool maintenance, and pool rentals. Our goal is to provide a safe, fun, and enjoyable environment for everyone to experience the joys of swimming.

Whether you're a beginner just starting out, or an experienced swimmer we have a program that is tailored to meet your needs.

Services

We offer a wide range of services designed to help individuals of all level

Splash Academy

With years of experience and a team of certified instructors, we are confident in our ability to teach people of all ages and abilities the life-saving skill of swimming. Our swim lessons are personalized and designed to meet the individual needs and goals of each student.

Rent-a-pool

Our Rent a Pool service provides everything needed for an enjoyable pool day without the responsibilities and expenses of owning one. Whether you're celebrating a birthday, or simply looking to relax by the pool, Rent a Pool has everything needed to make it a memorable experience.

Pool Maintenance

We understand that taking care a pool can be a difficult task, which is why we are offering our professional pool maintenance services. Our team of experienced and knowledgeable technicians will ensure that your pool stays in top condition all year round, giving you the peace of mind to relax and enjoy it.

Splash Community

We believe that water activities should be both fun and educational. That's why we offer a variety of Splash community programs designed to entertain and engage participants of all ages. Our programs are ideal for schools, summer camps, water parties, lovers and friends hangout and community events.
